Equity Research Competiton

The Stock Summit is an annual flagship event organized by the Capital Market Student Club (CMSC) at the University of Indonesia. It focuses on empowering young people interested in the capital markets through a series of competitions, seminars, and professional networking sessions.

The goal of Stock Summit 2025 is to create an environment that fosters the development of analytical skills, strategic understanding, and innovation in the financial sector. The event aims to cultivate future leaders ready to contribute to the transformation of the financial industry.

Stock Summit 2025 akan menghadirkan satu kompetisi, yaitu equity research competition.s Kompetisi ini dirancang untuk mengasah keterampilan analisis pasar, strategi investasi, dan ketajaman riset keuangan dengan mempertimbangkan data dan fakta lapangan sehingga dapat menghasilkan hasil keputusan investasi yang layak dipertimbangkan. Kompetisi berfungsi sebagai wadah bagi mahasiswa untuk menunjukkan potensi dan daya saing mereka di pasar modal, sekaligus mendorong kolaborasi dan inovasi antar peserta.
